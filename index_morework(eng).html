Über Mich

Hi, ich bin Elias! Ich bin Filmstudent an der Fakultät Design der TH Nürnberg. Dort studiere ich aktuell im fünften Semester und habe neben Film auch die Fächer Cast (Webcontent), Fotografie, Typografie und Interaktionsdesign belegt. Neben einigen Kino- und Fernsehfilmen war ich bereits an vielen Werbefilmproduktionen und an einem Dokumentarfilm als Kameramann, Cutter und Produzent tätig.

Arbeiten

Documentary The Smile of Furuflaten One Last Race
Tom Frode Johansen is a legend among the dog sledding community. We‘re following him through the 1200km of one of the toughest sled dog races in the world, which not even someone with his experience is guaranteed to pass.
Short Action 3 Shots
Two childhood friends meet again by chance at a bar. What one of the two doesn‘t know: The other one is on the run.
Corporate Ironman Triathlon
Over the course of last three years (and counting), we documented almost twenty Ironman triathlon races as the official film crew. We have covered all the german events as well as a number of other european ones, like Cascais in Portugal, Wales in the UK and Luxembourg.
Music Video Life on Mars
„Life on Mars“ is a socio-critical song by berlin-based rapper Bohl about our modern society and that not even a „life on mars“ will be able to save us anymore.
Corporate Marathon Frankfurt
Since end of 2024, I have been documenting the running events of Spiridon Frankfurt, ranging from shorter, 10 km runs up to full marathons.
Corporate IHK Wahlspot
Nurembergs chamber of industry and commerce had an important election in 2024. To increase voter turnout, we produced a TV spot.
Documentary Pullman City Quest Sled Dog Racing in Germany
This sled dog race in the western town of Pullman City in the Oberharz is probably one of the most atmospheric in Germany. We were able to accompany and document this event on film as part of our project work in the 3rd semester at the TH Nuremberg Faculty of Design.
Documentary Ki'Ki' Cave Project
What‘s the link between geologists and microbiologists on the island of Hawaii and NASAs space program? We were able to find out during our documentary project about how Hawaii‘s cave systems can help us find life on other planets.
Corporate IHK Wahlspot
A short portrait about Solomon Solgit, an acrobat and musician from Ethiopia, who has worked with numerous prestigious circuses like the Circque du Soleil and now works with children in Germany, where he teaches them acrobatics and music.
Corporate Marathon Frankfurt
The Marathon Frankfurt Association hosts a bunch of different running events in Frankfurt. From late 2024, I have been the main Videographer in all of these Events.
Documentary Solomon
A short portrait about Solomon Solgit, an acrobat and musician from Ethiopia, who has worked with numerous prestigious circuses like the Circque du Soleil and now works with children in Germany, where he teaches them acrobatics and music.

Music Video Wir hatten Alles
„Wir hatten Alles“ is a song by austrian singer-songwriter Clara Carmina, which was produced by a friend of mine with whom I‘ve worked on numerous projects before, for which he composed the music.
Corporate Gridless Nairobi
For a quite special corporate project we got to travel to Nairobi, Kenya, to produce a Video about Gridless, who are mining Bitcoin in Kenya sustainably and with the needs of the community in mind.
Corporate Pax8 Beyond Conference Aftermovie
Pax8 is a leading player in the network- and software-industry. Every year, they host the „Beyond“-Conference with attendees from all over the world of the likes of Microsoft, LastPass, etc. We got to produce their 2024 Aftermovie.
